 Process

How It Works

Getting assistance is simple, transparent, and designed with you in mind. Follow these easy steps to submit your request.

Congressional Office
Occidental Mindoro · Serving Since 2024
1
Step One
Create an Account
Register using your personal information to create a secure resident account in the system.
2
Step Two
Submit Your Request
Choose the type of assistance you need, fill in the form, and attach the required documents online.
3
Step Three
Wait for Review
The Congressional Office staff will review your eligibility and documents within the processing period.
4
Step Four
Track & Receive
Monitor your request status live. Once approved, you'll be notified for the release of your assistance.
